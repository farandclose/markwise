# Nimbus - Product Brief

## Overview

Nimbus is a collaborative notebook for small product teams who live in markdown. It ships by <!-- mw:n1 -->Q3<!-- /mw:n1 --> of next year.<!-- mw:n2 -->

The core promise is simple: <!-- mw:n3 -->write once, review anywhere<!-- /mw:n3 -->. Reviewers comment inline, and an agent applies the edits.

## Market

The market is <!-- mw:n4 -->large and growing<!-- /mw:n4 -->. Our wedge is teams already drafting specs in plain text.

## Pricing

We will charge a flat <!-- mw:n5 -->$12 per seat<!-- /mw:n5 --> per month, billed annually.

## Timeline

A private beta opens in spring, with general availability to follow.<!-- mw:n6 -->

## Open Risks

Adoption hinges on the editor integrations landing on schedule.
